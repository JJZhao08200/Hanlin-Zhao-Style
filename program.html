Hackathon · Analytics 2025

UBC Sauder and Google Hackathon with Google Cloud Tools

End-to-end YouTube analytics system combining APIs, ML forecasting, and interactive dashboards.

My role
API integration · Forecasting (ARIMA/LSTM) · Dashboard build
Impact
Real-time reporting + trend prediction to support growth decisions
YouTube Analytics API OAuth YouTube Data API v3 ARIMA LSTM Looker Studio

What we did

We successfully integrated the YouTube Analytics API to retrieve key metrics such as views, subscriber growth, audience distribution, and traffic sources, while resolving OAuth authentication challenges to ensure secure data access.

Since the API only allows access to our own channel data, we leveraged YouTube Data API v3 for public statistics and applied Machine Learning models, including time series forecasting (ARIMA, LSTM), to predict future trends.

Additionally, we integrated Looker Studio to create real-time dashboards for visualizing YouTube performance, enhancing insights through interactive and data-driven reporting.

What’s next

Moving forward, we aim to refine our ML models, automate data collection, and build an intelligent analytics system that seamlessly combines API data, predictive modeling, and visualization for optimized YouTube growth strategies.

Hackathon · Climate Finance 2025

UBC Climate Innovation Challenge Hackathon

Project finance strategy for Deep Sky — a large-scale Direct Air Capture (DAC) facility in Québec.

My role
Project finance structuring · Risk assessment · Model assumptions & DSCR logic
Impact
Clear bankability narrative + financing options for decision discussions
Project Finance Offtake Carbon Markets Risk Management DSCR Capital Structure

Over two days, I worked with an incredible team to tackle a real-world project finance challenge—structuring the financing strategy for Deep Sky, a large-scale Direct Air Capture (DAC) facility in Québec.

  • Climate Finance & Offtake Agreements – Explored sustainability-linked bonds, debt structuring, and revenue stability.
  • Carbon Markets & Risk Management – Assessed the feasibility of carbon credit pricing, voluntary/compliance markets, and offtake strategies.
  • Financial Modeling & Investment Structuring – Evaluated project bankability, DSCR, and equity/debt allocation to optimize returns.

This experience deepened my understanding of sustainable finance, project structuring, and carbon markets, reinforcing the role of finance in accelerating climate innovation. Grateful for the insights from industry experts and my teammates!

Hackathon · Learning Analytics 2024

Learning Analytics Hackathon (Team HAX)

Award-winning dashboards to improve engagement quality and reduce disengaged “spam” posts in online learning.

My role
Metric design · Dashboard build · Data analysis (Python/Tableau) · Storytelling
Impact
Won “Most Relevant to Teaching and Learning” for actionable instructor/student views
Python Tableau Data Analysis Web Design Engagement Metrics

During the recent Learning Analytics Hackathon, I participated as part of Team HAX, where we won the "Most Relevant to Teaching and Learning" award. Our project aimed to promote meaningful discussions aligned with learning outcomes while reducing the number of disengaged or "spam" posts in online learning environments. We proposed dashboards for both instructors and students to track engagement and ensure that discussions contributed to the intended learning objectives.

Throughout the process, I applied various technical skills, including Python, Tableau, data analysis, and web design, which were instrumental in creating the dashboards and analyzing data to provide actionable insights. These combined efforts led to a solution that enhanced the overall learning experience and contributed to our success in the competition.
