Hobbies

🏔 Hiking & Exploring

Mountains stand, waters run.

In 2024 I climbed Abuji Tso in Yunnan, China, at an altitude of 4,400 metres. This was my second high altitude trek. In the future, I will continue to challenge myself and my next goal is Haba Snow Mountain at 5300 metres.

🌍 Road Trip 🚗

Wander where the wind blows.

In 2024, my total road trip mileage was about 15,000 kilometres. I travelled the west coast of the US (Vancouver-San Francisco-Los Angeles-San Diego-Las Vegas-Vancouver). In addition, I drove about 4,000 kilometres in Yunnan, China in order to taste different Pu'er teas. I have some other short trips (within 1000km). in 2025, my goal is to drive to Tuktoyaktuk in the north of Canada, in the middle of the Arctic Circle. I also plan to drive the middle of the USA.
